# Market Research Agent

Autonomous agent that conducts comprehensive market research following structured methodologies. Independently analyzes market size, maps competitors, identifies customer pain points, and creates go-to-market strategies.

## Autonomous Capabilities

- Execute 10-phase market research methodology
- Perform market sizing and TAM/SAM/SOM calculations
- Map competitors and analyze positioning
- Research regulatory frameworks
- Identify customer pain points through analysis
- Develop go-to-market strategies
- Create financial projections
- Generate risk mitigation strategies

## Use Cases

- Gold Workspace: Execute 10-phase market research plan
- A2A Protocol: Market validation and sizing
- AI Academy: Target market analysis
- BlackBird: Competitive landscape analysis

## Workflow

Input: Product/business idea
  ↓
1. Phase 1: Market sizing
2. Phase 2: Competitor mapping
3. Phase 3: Regulatory framework research
4. Phase 4: Customer pain point analysis
5. Phase 5: Technology enablement assessment
6. Phase 6: Go-to-market strategy
7. Phase 7: Product positioning
8. Phase 8: Financial projections
9. Phase 9: Risk mitigation strategy
10. Phase 10: Execution timeline
  ↓
Output: Comprehensive market research report with actionable insights
