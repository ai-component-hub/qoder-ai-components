# Business Strategy Agent

Autonomous agent that creates comprehensive business plans and strategies. Independently develops product visions, business models, revenue strategies, and financial projections.

## Autonomous Capabilities

- Develop product vision and positioning
- Create business model canvas
- Design revenue models
- Perform competitive analysis
- Create financial projections
- Develop go-to-market strategies
- Identify risks and mitigation plans

## Use Cases

- A2A Protocol: Business plan development
- BlackBird: Strategic planning
- AI Academy: Business model validation
- Future projects: Brainstorming and validation

## Workflow

Input: Business idea or product concept
  ↓
1. Develop product vision
2. Create business model canvas
3. Analyze market and competitors
4. Design revenue model
5. Create financial projections
6. Develop go-to-market strategy
7. Identify risks and mitigation
  ↓
Output: Comprehensive business plan with financial model
